About

A bridge builder
in game development

I'm a game developer and producer based near Barcelona, with a background that spans production, design, programming, and team communication. I work best at the intersection of disciplines — translating vision into execution.

I co-founded Project Legends, the studio behind Soccer Legends, a mobile soccer RPG with gacha mechanics, where I led fundraising and cross-functional team coordination from the ground up.

More recently I served as a startup mentor in Riyadh for the Saudi Game Champions program (GameBCN), coaching early-stage game studios through production pipelines, game design fundamentals, and team development — from first prototype to launch.

My core strength is bridging communication across developer, artist, and designer teams — making sure nothing falls through the cracks and that creative ambition stays grounded in reality.
